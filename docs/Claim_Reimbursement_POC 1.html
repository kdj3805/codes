Automated Claim Eligibility & Reimbursement Engine

Claims teams currently spend significant time manually checking medical invoices, hospital bills, scans/tests, and pharmacy bills against policy documents to decide what is reimbursable. This is slow, opaque for customers, and prone to errors.

This POC creates an AI copilot for claim reimbursement. It ingests all claim artefacts — invoices, admission / discharge summaries, scan & test reports, pharmacy / medicine bills — and combines them with the member’s policy document (e.g., HDFC policy PDF) to automatically:

  • Extract structured data from unstructured PDFs / images using OCR + LLM extraction
  • Retrieve and interpret relevant policy clauses for coverage, sub-limits, co-pay, waiting periods, and exclusions
  • Apply time-window constraints (e.g., tests from 2 months prior to 1 month post admission, medicine bills within allowed period)
  • Compute the eligible vs non-eligible amount by line item and summarise the final approved reimbursement

Output is a transparent reimbursement summary for both claim operations and customers — clearly explaining “what is paid, what is not, and why”.
